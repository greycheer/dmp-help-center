#### 2.2.2 Product Display

**Product Display** is used to configure how the device appears in the App/Web interface, including:

- Device model name and description
- Product images and icons
- Product specifications and parameters

**Configuration Steps:**

1. Navigate to **Product → Product Development**
2. Click **Continue Development** for the target product
3. Select the **Device Interaction** subpage
4. Click **Product Display** to configure

**Note:** Product images should be in PNG or JPG format, with recommended dimensions of 800x800 pixels.
